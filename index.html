About This Collection

Fyodor Dostoevsky (1821-1881) was a Russian novelist, philosopher, and journalist. His works explore the depths of human psychology, morality, and spirituality. These timeless masterpieces continue to inspire readers worldwide.

This collection brings together all of Dostoevsky's major works in one beautiful place, making it easy to explore his profound literary legacy.
